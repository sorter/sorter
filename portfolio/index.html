easytag

EasyTag is a cloud-based software which offers businesses a unified perspective of the current status of any inbound or outbound order. At the core of EasyTag is a system driven by tagging virtually everything including shipments, invoices, purchases orders and merchandise. By merely scanning these tags, company members are able to efortlessly log their progress on filling any given orders. Behind the screens, EasyTag has a robust network of backend services which monitor and analyze the state of its user's orders.


At EasyTag my core responsibilities involve driving feature development and assuring availability as well as reliability of our service's cloud backend infastructure. Our services are deployed primarily on Linux-based servers and are distributed on the Amazon Web Services platform. In addition to our server-side offerings, I have also driven development of the native EasyTag Android client as well as the web-application offering.

Fathom

Fathom is a record label and distributor dedicated to releasing and distributing quality, new and innovative music. Fathom was founded in 2009 as a distributor based in Toronto, Canada and relaunched in 2014 as a label with an ambitious programme of cd, vinyl and digital releases.


Since its conception, I have carrier out all of Fathom's technical endeavours. This involved writing a customized ecommerce solution to facilitate online sales of tangible as well as non-tangible goods. The proprietor of Fathom made it a top priority for the company to utilize free and open source technologies to deploy their services online. As such, I was tasked with deploying several Linux servers to store the databases, website, enterprise email accounts as well as their online shop.
